Last Friday was my last day at StarMaker, and beginning next Tuesday, I am proud to be joining the team at URX.


After having stayed a while at StarMaker, and learned a lot about B2C mobile apps, I am excited to join a different kind of product, this time more engineering and science focused. If you don't know about URX, I suggest you check them out, they are revolutionizing the way users navigate the tree structure that is mobile apps.
I am thrilled to join a team working on such an innovative product that - I believe - will change the way people use smartphones.


The challenges ahead seem terribly exciting, and this time I won't only be fooling around in Python, but also with Go, Scala, and many other cool technologies. I've been looking for a good excuse to mess with these for too long, so this is fantastic news!

Thank you StarMaker and all the people there, especially some engineers - who will recognize themselves - for all the things I learned, all the things I broke, and all the ones I fixed.


On to the next adventure!
